Clamilton Estate

Plane — Clamhattan

If a wordy permanent entering the battlefield would cause an ability of that permanent or another wordy permanent to trigger, that ability triggers an additional time. (A permanent is wordy if it has four or more lines of rules text.)
Whenever Chaos ensues, exile cards from the top of your library until you exile a wordy nonland card, Put that card into your hand. Put each card not cast this way on the bottom of your library in a random order.

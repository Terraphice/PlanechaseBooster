Circus of the Sun

Plane — Las Vegas

Whenever you planeswalk here or at the beginning of your upkeep, create two 1/1 red performer creature tokens with flying and haste.
Whenever one or more creatures you control with flying deal combat damage to a player, you may return it to your hand. If you do, draw cards equal to its power.
Whenever Chaos ensues, put a flying counter on up to one target creature. It becomes a Performer in addition to its other types.

We Hope You Like Squirrels

Plane — Secret Lair

At the beginning of each player's upkeep and whenever a player casts a spell, that player creates a 1/1 green Squirrel creature token.

Whenever chaos ensues, put a +1/+1 counter on each Squirrel you control.

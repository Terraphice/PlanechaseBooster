Time Spiral

Phenomenon

When you encounter Time Spiral, each player exiles a nonland card from their hand or graveyard with time counters on it equal to its mana value. If a card exiled this way doesn’t have suspend, it gains suspend.

*(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

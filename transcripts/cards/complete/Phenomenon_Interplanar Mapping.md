Interplanar Mapping

Phenomenon

When you planeswalk away from Interplanar Mapping, starting with you, each player takes turns planeswalking until a phenomenon is encountered or they can’t planeswalk again. *(Due to a planar ability, or any other reason.)*
If an ability of a plane would trigger while planeswalking this way, that ability triggers an  additional time.

*(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

Thraben, High City

Plane — Innistrad

When you planeswalk to Thraben, High City, it becomes night.

Whenever it becomes night, each player may transform any number of Humans, Horrors, Werewolves, and Vampires they control.

{CHAOS}: Whenever you roll {CHAOS}, it becomes night, and you may create X 2/2 black Zombie creature tokens with decayed, where X is equal to the number of creatures that died this turn.

Atlas Consultation

Phenomenon

When you encounter Atlas Consultation, starting with you, each player votes for a plane card visited this game. Reveal cards from the top of the planar deck until you reveal a card with the most votes or tied for the most votes. Put that card on top and the rest on the bottom in a random order. (A plane card becomes visited when it becomes the active plane or if it was the active plane at the start of the game.)
(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)

Artist: Alexander Forssberg
Set: Planechase Booster — Terraphice
Rarity: N/A

The Dead Zone

Plane — Ulgrotha

When you planeswalk to The Dead Zone, players lose all unspent mana. Players can’t add or produce mana, or activate mana abilities.

Whenever you roll {CH}, create X 1/1 black Vampire creature tokens with lifelink and flying, where X is the number of lands you control.

High and Dry Black Market

Phenomenon

♦ — Create a Treasure Token.
♦ ♦ — Draw a card.
♦ ♦ ♦ — Create a 3/2 colorless Shapeshifter creature token with Changeling.

*(Then Planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

Lotus Basin

Plane — Dominaria

When you planeswalk to Lotus Basin, and at the beginning of each upkeep, put a petal counter on Lotus Basin.

Whenever you roll {CH}, add X mana of any one color, where X is the number of petal counters on Lotus Basin.

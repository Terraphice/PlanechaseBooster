Great Desparking

Phenomenon

When you encounter Great Desparking, each {PLANESWALKER} roll of the planar die is a {CHAOS} roll until each player has rolled {PLANESWALKER} at least once.
Each planeswalker loses all abilities and becomes a creature with power and toughness each equal to the number of loyalty counters on it.

*(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

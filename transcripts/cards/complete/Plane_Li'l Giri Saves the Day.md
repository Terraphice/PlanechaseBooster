Li'l Giri Saves the Day

Plane — Secret Lair

Legendary Food tokens you control have hexproof and indestructible.

Whenever chaos ensues, put X +1/+1 counters on target legendary artifact creature you control, where X is the number of times you've rolled the planar die this turn.

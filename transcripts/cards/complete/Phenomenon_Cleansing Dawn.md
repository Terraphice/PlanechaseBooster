Cleansing Dawn

Phenomenon

When you encounter Cleansing Dawn, if it’s night, it becomes day.
Exile all Vampires, Werewolves, Zombies, and Horrors. For each creature exiled this way, its controller creates a 1/1 white Spirit creature token with flying, gains 1 life, and draws a card.
*(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

Fateful Return

Phenomenon

When you encounter Fateful Return, reveal cards from the top of the planar deck until you reveal the first plane card visited this game. Put that card on top and the rest on the bottom in a random order. *(A plane card becomes visited when it becomes the active plane or if it was the active plane at the start of the game.)
(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

Corrupting Dusk

Phenomenon

When you encounter Corrupting Dusk, if it’s day, it becomes night.
Exile all Angels, Elves, Merfolk, and Humans. For each creature exiled this way, its controller creates a 2/2 black Zombie Horror creature token with decayed, loses 1 life, and draws a card.
*(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*

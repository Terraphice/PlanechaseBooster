Omenpath Shortcut

Phenomenon

When you encounter Omenpath Shortcut, reveal the top six cards of the planar deck and choose a plane card from among the revealed cards. Put the chosen card on top and the rest on the bottom in a random order.

*(Then planeswalk away from this phenomenon.)*
